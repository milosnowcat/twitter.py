About

Hexagon

Hexagon is an online platform designed to help recent graduate students find their first job in their field of study and gain valuable work experience. We believe that talented students deserve meaningful professional opportunities, and we are committed to helping them take their first step in their careers.

Our platform is built on the idea that the connection between students and companies can be made easier and more effective. Hexagon acts as a social network that allows students to connect with companies, participate in practical projects, and develop practical skills while still in university. We believe that work experience is crucial for professional success, and we want to provide students with a way to obtain it in an accessible and relevant manner.

On Hexagon, students can create professional profiles, search for job opportunities, participate in projects proposed by companies, and connect with other students and industry professionals. Our platform uses intelligent algorithms to recommend job opportunities that align with students' skills, interests, and preferences, enabling them to find opportunities that fit their professional goals.

In addition, Hexagon also offers companies the ability to connect with talented and promising students seeking employment. Companies can post projects, search for student profiles, and establish relationships with potential candidates. We believe that Hexagon is a win-win solution for both students and companies, as it helps fulfill the needs of both sides.

At Hexagon, we are committed to the privacy and security of our users. We maintain high standards of data protection and strive to create a safe and trustworthy environment for students and companies to interact.

We are proud to offer Hexagon as a tool to help recent graduate students in their job search and professional development. Our team is constantly working on improving and expanding our platform to provide an exceptional user experience and contribute to the success of our users.

Thank you for choosing Hexagon and being part of our community! We look forward to helping you on your journey to a successful professional career!
